Woj Bombs

Adrian Wojnarowski (@wojespn) is the most popular NBA reporter in the business. Currently, he has 4.5 million followers on twitter. People often refer to his tweets as "Woj Bombs" because they are so nuclear. When he tweets, the NBA world listens. I was interested in pulling his tweets and seeing the most popular. I used Twitter's API to do this. With their API, I could only retrieve his last 3,200 tweets, which dates back to June 2019. I used Tableau for the visualizations below.

Below is a visualization of Woj's tweets and favorites per tweet. A favorite on Twitter is similar to a like on Facebook, it indicates how popular and engaging a tweet is. I only included "pure" tweets (no images/videos/links). There is also a filter to exclude any tweets with less than 10,000 favorites. Hover over each point to see the details.

I was also interested if certain words were more popular. Certain teams like the Lakers and Warriors, as well as certain players like Lebron and Kevin Durant, showed up as expected. Some surprises that were unique to 2020 were the Jazz and the word 'positive'. The Jazz were the first team with a positive case of Covid-19, which ended up suspending the season.
